    About Thalassemia

    Thalassemia is another abnormal kind of blood disease which is more severe when compared to sickle cell disease. The major cause of thalassemia is mutation in the DNA of cells carriers from parents to children. Thalassemia is having two kinds of diseases (i.e. Alpha and Beta Thalassemia). The life span of thalassemia cells is approximately 100 days. The symptoms of thalassemia can be observed after 6 months in new born babies because after 6 months normal hemoglobin starts replacing the fetal type. Symptoms are lack of energy, tiredness, breathing problem, pale skin (jaundice), irregular heartbeats.

    Alpha thalassemia: We need 4 alpha-globin protein chains, each individual, two on each chromosome 16. We get two from each parent. If any one of the gene is missing then alpha thalassemia occurs.

    Beta thalassemia: We need 2 globin genes for beta-globin chains, one from each parent. If one or both genes are not matched, beta thalassemia will occur.

    About project :

    Sickle cells is hematological disorder(hematological is a study of blood in health and diseases) which may leads to organ damage, heart strokes, serious complications. It may also reduces the human life span. Most of the sickle cells are observed in new born babies. It was initially thought to be a particular feature of tribal peoples, but it has now been found in all populations. Sickle cell Symptoms are observed in human beings as episodes of pains(crisis), Painful swelling of hands and Feet, and Vision problems. By using Artificial neural network technique we can identify sickle cells in human body effectively with high accuracy. Detecting sickle cell as early as possible which helps the patient to cure by using antibiotics, vitamins, blood transfusions, pain-relieving medicines etc.

    From all classifiers, we have achieved best accuracy from Random forest classifier(i.e. 96.3%) .
    we have stored RandomForest classifier trained data with the help of pickling package(i.e.pkl), by using pickling package we can predict without training the model continuously

    3) To improve the accuracy and performance of the system using Machine learning techniques we can compared to the traditional manual assessment and classification
